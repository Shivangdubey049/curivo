M.S Dhoni : The Untold Story

film by Mathur Goswami , Neeraj Pandey

The untold story of Mahendra Singh Dhoni's journey from ticket collector to trophy collector - the world-cup-winning captain of the Indian Cricket Team.

Characters

M.S Dhoni

Sushant Singh Rajput

MS Dhoni’s character transcends the boundaries of the cricket field. Known for his calm, composed leadership and sharp decision-making, from his early struggles in Ranchi to his rise as one of the most successful captains in world cricket. Uncover the psychological traits that made him a master at handling pressure, the influence of his childhood experiences. What drives his relentless pursuit of success? How does his quiet nature hide a fiercely competitive spirit? Dive into the character of MS Dhoni – a man who led with heart and never sought the limelight, yet became a symbol of inspiration for millions

Mr. Deval

Kumud Mishra

Mr. Deval is portrayed as a mentor and supportive figure who recognizes Dhoni’s potential, even when others doubt him. He is an important coach figure who pushes Dhoni to achieve more and is one of the first to notice Dhoni’s talent, even before he became a national sensation. Mr. Deval's encouragement and belief in Dhoni's abilities help guide him through the early stages of his cricketing career. Mr. Deval is a symbol of the unsung heroes who shape the careers of future legends. His quiet support, without seeking the limelight, reflects the important behind-the-scenes influence that coaches and mentors can have on a player’s life.

Sakshi Singh Dhoni Rawat

Sakshi Rawat was born in Dehradun, India, and they first met in school. However, they only reconnected much later when MS Dhoni was already an established cricketer. The two shared a friendship during their school days, but their romantic relationship blossomed when they met again in Kolkata while Dhoni was touring with the Indian cricket team. Sakshi has been a constant pillar of support for Dhoni, particularly during the high-pressure years of his career. While Dhoni's on-field persona is calm and composed, it’s clear that off the field, Sakshi provides him with emotional grounding and a sense of normalcy.
